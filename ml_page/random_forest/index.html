Random Forest

Machine learning with Random Forest, and standardization of data and GridSearchCV.

Random Forest models construct multiple ‘decision trees’; a single decision tree will look for one pattern or factor in the input data, and will make a decision. A forest may have hundreds of trees. The output is determined by a vote of all the trees in the forest.



Baseline Accuracy

We achieved a baseline accuracy of 83%.

Standardization

Standardization of the data lowered our accuracy score to 81%.

GridSearchCV

We gave GridSearchCV a total of nine possible units for two parameters. Its job is to find the optimum combination of those units for maximum accuracy. GridSearchCV drastically improved our accuracy score to 90%.
