K Nearest Neighbors

Machine learning through K Nearest Neighbors (KNN) and with standardization.

What is KNN?

KNN works by finding the distance between a query and all the examples in the data, selecting the specified number examples (K) closest to the query, then votes for the most frequent label (in the case of classification) or averages the labels (in the case of regression).




Baseline Accuracy

We achieved a baseline accuracy of 78% without any standardization.

Standardization

Standardization of the data and setting the number of neighbors to 11 increased our accuracy score to 88% or by 10%
